Welcome to the experiment!

In this study, you'll see words of colors (RED, GREEN, BLUE, YELLOW). Your job is to identify the color of the word's font (not the color that the word refers to). You'll do this by clicking on the circle at the bottom that matches the font's color. Note that the words may say something different (the word may be GREEN, but the font in this case is red). Since your job is to select the color of the font, the correct answer would be clicking on the red circle, not the green circle. The next word will appear after you click on the correct answer. Please be as fast as possible.
